**公式或評分規則摘要：**

Male patients:

6MWD in meters = (7.57 × height in cm) – (5.02 × age) – (1.76 × weight in kg) – 309

Lower limit of normal = 6MWD - 153

Female patients:

6MWD in meters = (2.11 × height in cm) – (2.29 × weight in kg)  – (5.78 × age) + 667

Lower limit of normal = 6MWD - 139
